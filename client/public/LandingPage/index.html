Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is this e-learning platform about?

Our platform is designed to empower rural communities by providing access to quality education in modern farming practices, digital skills, and entrepreneurship to help create sustainable livelihoods.

Who can join the training programs?

Anyone from rural areas with a desire to learn and grow is welcome! Whether you're a farmer, student, homemaker, or aspiring entrepreneur, our courses are made for all levels.

What topics are covered in the courses?

We cover modern farming techniques, organic farming, use of agri-tech tools, basic computer skills, smartphone usage, financial literacy, and starting small-scale businesses.

Do I need internet access to use the platform?

Yes, a basic internet connection is needed to access the learning content. However, we're working on downloadable modules for areas with low connectivity.

Is the platform available in regional languages?

Absolutely! We provide content in multiple regional languages to make learning more comfortable and accessible for all learners.

Are the courses free of cost?

Yes, all our courses are completely free to ensure that every learner in rural areas has equal access to quality education and skill development.
